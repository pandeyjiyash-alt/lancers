Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a freelance web development project cost?

Project costs depend on scope and complexity. A landing page typically starts from $500–$1,500. A full web application or SaaS product ranges from $3,000 to $20,000+. We provide a detailed, itemised quote after the discovery call — no guesswork, no hidden fees.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

Both! we've helped early-stage founders validate ideas with an MVP in 4 weeks, and we've worked with growing companies to scale their infrastructure to handle millions of users. Whatever stage you're at, there's a solution that fits your needs and budget.

Can you work with Our existing team or codebase?

Absolutely. We collaborate well with in-house developers, designers, and product managers. we're comfortable onboarding into existing codebases and following your team's conventions and workflows.

What happens after the project is delivered?

We offer a 30-day post-launch support period on all projects. After that, we can arrange a monthly retainer for ongoing maintenance, updates, and feature development. You'll never be left without support.

Do you sign NDAs and provide contracts?

Yes. All projects are covered by a formal contract that outlines deliverables, timelines, and payment terms. We're happy to sign your NDA before discussing any proprietary details.
