Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Vantade provide?

Vantade specialises in web application development, SaaS platforms, custom dashboards, mobile apps, API design, cloud infrastructure, and DevOps solutions. We tailor our services to meet your specific business needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. MVP development typically takes 6–12 weeks, while enterprise solutions may take 3–6 months. We provide detailed estimates after understanding your requirements.

Do you work with distributed teams?

Yes, we are a fully remote team and collaborate seamlessly with clients worldwide. We use modern communication tools and maintain transparent delivery rhythms with regular updates and demos.

What technologies do you specialise in?

We work with React, Next.js, Vue.js, Python, Django, PHP, Laravel, Node.js, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and more. Our team adapts to your tech stack requirements.

How do you ensure code quality and security?

We follow industry best practices including code reviews, automated testing, security audits, OWASP compliance, and continuous integration/deployment. Quality assurance is embedded in every project phase.

What is your support and maintenance process?

We offer post-launch support, bug fixes, performance optimisation, and feature enhancements. Custom support plans are available based on your needs and requirements.
