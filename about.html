The Person Behind the Work

Vantade is a freelance digital studio built on a simple belief: great software should be accessible to every business, not just those with enterprise budgets. With over five years of hands-on experience building products that are used by real people, we've developed a no-nonsense approach to digital work — clear scope, fair pricing, and delivery you can count on.

The Journey

Key milestones that shaped Vantade into what it is today.

2020 — Getting Started

First Freelance Projects

Began freelancing while completing a computer science degree. Built first three client projects — a restaurant website, a small e-commerce store, and an internal HR tool. Learned the hard way how important scope clarity and communication are.

2021 — Growth Phase

Expanded into Full-Stack & SaaS

Delivered 25+ projects across web and mobile. Began specialising in SaaS product development and took on first US client. Revenue tripled. Invested heavily in mastering cloud architecture and modern frontend frameworks.

2022 — AI Integration Era

Embracing Artificial Intelligence

Early adopter of GPT-3 and LangChain for client projects. Built first AI chatbot for a UK e-commerce client — it reduced their support tickets by 60%. Started offering AI integration as a core service offering.

2023 — Scale & Recognition

50+ Clients, Global Reach

Hit the milestone of 50 completed client projects. Received Top Rated recognition on Upwork. Launched Vantade as a standalone brand to better serve clients who prefer working outside freelancing platforms.

2024–2026 — Today

100+ Projects, Enterprise Clients

Serving clients from startups to funded scaleups. Specialising in AI-first products, cloud-native architectures, and performance-critical applications. 98% client satisfaction rate across all engagements.
